=======
A location-based discovery platform to explore India’s hidden places, cafés, cultural spots, and local events.

Enables locals and experienced travelers to contribute accurate, map-based recommendations and insider tips.

Real-time community reviews focused on authenticity, safety, cost, and best time to visit.

Smart category and interest-based filters (Nature, Culture, Food, Events, Hangouts) for personalized discovery.

Built-in weekend planner to create quick, nearby trip plans based on time, budget, and location.

Bookmark feature to save favorite spots and plans for future visits.
